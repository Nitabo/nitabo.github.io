Work Ethics

I strongly believe that every design decision impact our users’ lives. The web is everywhere nowadays, conditioning our access to democracy and education, to entertainment and information. It is our duty as "Web Makers" to consider the broader consequences of our work and engage in ethical design to ensure that the tools we create empower an impartial use of the power of Internet. I am currently writing a thesis to illustrate my views. Building a safe, fair and inclusive place is my motto and I wish to spread awareness among my peer developers and my clients. Here is my manifesto.

" Be the change you wish to see in the world " - Mahatma Gandhi

Manifesto

  • I will build a legal and ethical project:
    • I will guide my client all along the project
    • I will ensure a trusting and fruitful designer-client relationship
    • I will follow strict and comprehensive project guidelines
  • I will be considerate of other parties:
    • I will do my best to help and inform the end user
    • I will be considerate of my peer developers
    • I will mind the resource usage of my project
  • I will be complying with the law:
    • I will not be unlawfully and unexpectedly contacting the user
    • I will not unlawfully and unethically manipulate the user data
    • I will respect the copyright and intellectual property laws
  • I will be a honest and moral developer:
    • I will not manipulate the user’s opinion or try to take their money
    • I will not make money out of the user’s data or out of their resources
    • I will not fall for easy scams to get traffic, popularity or to gain money
  • I will be working for accessibility and inclusion:
    • I will be working for disability and impairment with accessible design and content
    • I will design with technology disparities in mind, in terms of technology and bandwidth
    • I will design for social inclusion, no mater the income or location
    • I will design for all education levels
  • I will be working towards inclusive representation:
    • I will not enforce racist bias and I will work towards inclusive cultural representation
    • I will not enforce sexist bias and I will work towards inclusive women representation
    • I will not enforce unhealthy beauty standards and I will work towards inclusive body representation
