Who we are

Founded in the Midwest, we are a human-centered company, and we focus on innovation by technology.
In 2017, we reinvented ourselves and moved in the heart of Silicon Valley, California.

Our company name carries our mission to empower people and lead businesses into the intelligence age.

Our focus today is on autonomous systems to support human ingenuity with machine intelligence.

We provide built-in solutions to integrate Artificial Intelligence in your business.

We provide labs and cutting edge environments to help you become a leader in AI.

Making a difference

We strive to empower people & individuals with technology

Our credo is "People First" and we aim to maintain a human-centered approach.

We strive to help businesses realize their goals and ambitions

We focus on empowering people to build the future of businesses and communities.

We strive to make a positive difference in the community

We are passionate to make an impact and we exist to deliver value to society.

Meet our team

In this era of disruptive technologies shaping new businesses, people still come first.

Our team brings in the combined business and technology expertise to lead you into the intelligence revolution.

We rely on an ever-expanding group of experts working hand in hand, among them industry practitioners, hardware-software convergence experts, technology platform specialists, partners and consultants.

We are inspired by the future of autonomous technologies, but we stay grounded by building expertise.
