Robotics

Intelligent machines become much aware of the world around them as they talk to other internet enabled devices.

In a smart manufacturing factory floor, autonomous vehicles communicate with robotic arms or conveyors to take loading and unloading instructions without human intervention.

These autonomous vehicles can recognize objects or people while navigating shop floor stations, fulfillment centers, distribution centers, and warehouses.

The AI system competency is to develop a fully autonomous system stack by unifying the power of sensors with artificial intelligence.

Both deep learning at the cloud and machine learning optimization at the edge are implemented to get there.

Use cases for the industry 4.0 business are defined and deployed with such autonomous systems.
