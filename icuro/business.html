Autonomous platform

Our platform recognizes its operating context and responds accordingly, making autonomous decisions or providing data-driven recommendations to users.

It requires an extensive infrastructure of high-performance computing, and connectivity. The combination of contextual perception and sensor fusion lets our machines learn and engage in real time - seamlessly and intuitively.

This single product architecture combines both hardware and software. It means equipping enterprises with full systems, not only components, for new autonomous capabilities.

We implement an adaptive testing framework to self-correct and ensure reliability under various unanticipated circumstances.

Edge Intelligence platform

Our edge intelligence platform analyzes real time data from connected objects and delivers actionable intelligence at the edge of network, near the source of data.

This reduces the communication bandwidth required between sensors and the central data center by performing analytics near the source of data. As less data has to travel the network, less bottlenecks are to be expected, freeing up the infrastructure.

This hardware and software architecture includes a large number of independent nodes that can autonomously execute business computations, including security analytics.

We have skillfully chosen the right sensors, devices, and machines to operate in a secured network of connected objects.

Our platform is built to maintain support for cloud connectivity, offering services and applications for industrial connected objects, digital health, smart farming, and smart buildings.

Our platform integrates sophisticated technologies such as advanced analytics, security, and mobility on an edge computing device.

The edge intelligence platform can be configured locally but can also use the cloud.

Edge computing unlocks the business value of connected objects data in a secured framework.

We offer our tools and expertise to allow organizations to exploit the possibilities of the edge intelligence platform.
